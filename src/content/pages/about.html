Fast, Easy, and Affordable — Guaranteed!
Looking for professional electrical continuing education to renew your license? At Ohms Electrical CE, our state-approved online CE courses are carefully selected to ensure full accreditation, ease of access, and quality learning. Each course is designed to enhance your electrical knowledge, keep you up to date with industry standards, and help you renew your electrical license online with confidence.

Learn Anytime, Anywhere
We understand how busy life can get — that’s why Ohms Electrical CE brings education directly to you. Whether you’re at home, at the office, or on the go, our web-based learning platform works on any device with internet access and a standard web browser (such as Chrome, Firefox, or Edge). No complicated software required. Study at your own pace, on your own schedule, and from the comfort of your home, saving both time and money while earning your continuing education hours.

Course Approval
All Ohms Electrical CE courses are approved by your state’s regulatory board, ensuring you receive legitimate credit toward your license renewal.

Secure Payment
Your security matters. All payments are processed safely through PayPal, giving you peace of mind. Once you purchase your course, PayPal will send you a confirmation email verifying your secure transaction.
