  • Course Description

    This course offers a complete guide to the National Electrical Code (NEC) and meets all required Texas state regulations (TDLR). It is designed for electricians seeking to renew their license online and qualifies for license renewal as an Electrical Apprentice, Journeyman Electrician, Master Electrician, Journeyman Sign Electrician, Master Sign Electrician, Residential Wireman, or Maintenance Electrician. Gain the knowledge and credit you need quickly, conveniently, and in full compliance with state requirements.

    -One (1) hour of instruction regarding Rules/Laws (Recently Updated)
    -Two (2) hours of instruction regarding National Electric Code
    -One (1) hour of instruction regarding Safety NFPA 70E

  • Texas State Requirements

    Texas Electrician License Renewal and Continuing Education Requirements

    For each renewal, an electrical apprentice, journeyman electrician, master electrician, journeyman sign electrician, master sign electrician, residential wireman, or maintenance electrician must complete four hours of continuing education in:

    The National Electrical Code, or the current version of the National Electrical Code, as approved by the National Fire Protection Association (NFPA); State law and rules that regulate the conduct of licensees; and Safety as defined in the National Fire Protection Association (NFPA) 70E.
